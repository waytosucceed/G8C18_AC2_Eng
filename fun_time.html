Fun Time : Idiom Time


A Watched pot never boils: It doesn't help to continuously check on anything that takes time to complete. All you need to do is wait.
Use: A watched pot never boils so let your daughter be the way she is because she is an adult and she is studying medicine in the best college of the world.

Don’t put too many irons in the fire: Try not to multitask; instead, concentrate on one task at a time.
Use: Just give yourself a break for now and don’t put too many irons in the fire.
